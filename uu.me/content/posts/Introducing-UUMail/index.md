Today, we're introducing **UUMail** - an email alias and forwarding product built for safer signups, cleaner identity boundaries, and practical inbox control.

Email is still the key to most online accounts. That makes every signup a small trust decision: which address do you share, how much exposure do you accept, and what happens when a service leaks or starts sending unwanted mail?

UUMail is designed around a simple idea: your real inbox should not have to be your public identity everywhere.

---

## Why UUMail?

### **1. Aliases create safer boundaries**

Create addresses for different products, communities, or workflows. If an alias becomes noisy or risky, you can manage that alias without changing your real inbox.

### **2. Forwarding keeps the workflow familiar**

Messages can still arrive where you already read email. UUMail adds a privacy layer without forcing a new daily mail client.

### **3. Built with infrastructure in mind**

Behind the product are services for alias validation, forwarding policy, quotas, held mail, billing, analytics, and account lifecycle management.

---

## How It Works

1. Create an alias
2. Use it when signing up or sharing an address
3. UUMail validates and forwards matching mail
4. Manage aliases, quotas, and account settings from the dashboard

Simple, transparent, and fair.
